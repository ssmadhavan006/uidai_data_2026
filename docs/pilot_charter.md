# Pilot Charter
## Aadhaar Pulse - Child Update Acceleration Pilot

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-01-10  
**Duration:** 12 weeks

---

## 1. Pilot Objective

Validate the effectiveness of data-driven interventions in accelerating child biometric updates (ages 5-15) in high-priority districts.

---

## 2. Scope

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Treatment Districts | 5 (highest priority) |
| Control Districts | 5 (matched by state) |
| Duration | 12 weeks |
| Primary Intervention | Mobile Camps + Device Upgrades |

---

## 3. Selected Districts

### Treatment Group
Districts with highest priority scores receiving interventions.

### Control Group  
Matched districts receiving standard operations (no additional interventions).

See `outputs/pilot_regions.csv` for full list.

---

## 4. Success KPIs

| Metric | Baseline | Target | Measurement |
|--------|----------|--------|-------------|
| **bio_update_child** | Current rate | +30% | Weekly count |
| **completion_rate** | ~0.7 | >0.85 | Updates/Demand |
| **update_backlog** | Current | -50% | Queue size |

---

## 5. Evaluation Methodology

**Difference-in-Differences (DiD)**
- Compare pre/post changes between treatment and control
- Interaction coefficient = causal effect estimate
- Control for parallel trends assumption

---

## 6. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Spillover effects | Geographic separation of groups |
| Attrition | 12-week minimum commitment |
| External shocks | Control group comparison |

---

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|

| 3-4 | Intervention deployment |

| 11-12 | Evaluation & reporting |
